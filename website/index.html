Key Insights

Championship

India — Unbeaten Champions

India went 8-0 through the tournament — the only undefeated side. They beat England by 68 runs in the Semi-Final and clinched the Final vs South Africa by just 7 runs.

Toss Analysis

Field First Strategy

A majority of captains chose to field after winning the toss, reflecting the T20 trend of chasing. However, the data shows mixed outcomes across different venues.

Bowling

Afghanistan's Spin Web

Afghanistan's bowling unit — Rashid Khan, Naveen-ul-Haq and Fazalhaq Farooqi — dismantled multiple top sides and nearly caused an upset against South Africa in the Semis.

Batting

Power-Hitting Era

519 sixes were hit across 52 matches. Teams like West Indies and India led the six-hitting charts, reflecting the power-hitting philosophy of modern T20 cricket.

Venue

New York's Seaming Track

The drop-in pitches at Nassau County, New York produced seam-friendly conditions — leading to India's 7-wicket win over Pakistan, and the historic USA–Pakistan tie.

Upset

USA Holds Pakistan

Match 11 — USA vs Pakistan ended in a tie and USA won the Super Over. One of the biggest shocks in T20 World Cup history, showing the global growth of cricket.
